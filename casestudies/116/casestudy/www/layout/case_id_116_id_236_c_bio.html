Biography: Bob Ivry


 


Bob Ivry
Photo © PBS

Bob Ivry was an investigative reporter and editor for Bloomberg News. He was winner of numerous journalism awards, including the George Polk, the Gerald Loeb and the Hillman Prize. BusinessInsider.com named @bobivry one of "The 101 Finance People You Have to Follow on Twitter." Ivry had also written for Esquire, Washington Post Book World, Popular Science and Maxim, among other national publications.

Ivry describes how few reporters covered the Federal Reserve.
