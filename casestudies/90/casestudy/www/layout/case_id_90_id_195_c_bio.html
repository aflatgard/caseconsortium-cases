Biography: Carl Wade


Carl Wade

Carl Wade was 65 in 2011. He was imprisoned first in 1989 in Colorado, then transferred to California in 2000. In 2003, he was brought to the California Medical Facility in Vacaville. He had been a woodcutter. 

Carl Wade on his petition for compassionate release.
